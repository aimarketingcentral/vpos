◆ CeilingAmountPerCardType

string POSLink2.Manage.GetSafParametersRsp.CeilingAmountPerCardType
getset

Ceiling amount per card type. Once this amount is reached it will not accept any SAF record.

Separated by Card Type with space in the card type sequence of "Visa MasterCard AMEX Diners Discover JCB enRoute VisaFleet MasterCardFleet FleetOne Fleetwide Fuleman Gascard Voyager WrightExpress Extended(other) Interac CUP Maestro Sinclair. "2000 2000 2000 2000 2000 2000 2000 2000 2000 2000 2000 2000 2000 2000 2000 2000 2000 2000 2000 2000".

1 exception that value of "2000" without any spaces means taking 2000 as unified ceiling amount for each card type.

Empty value means ceiling amount validation will not be started during SAF.
